Problem

A beautiful space for all kinds of expressions and interactions has been created by technology era. Do we ever feel overwhelmed? Too many apps, too much personal exposure, too much distraction? Too many #livinginthemoment and little time for #memories and #reminiscing? No privacy. Time flies. People come and go. Nothing lasts. We fix it with Remo!

Idea

A Shared Interactive Journaling Tool with:
1. Personal Space
2. Customized Sharing
3. Quick access to the memory
4. Reconnection Booster

Research

The team recruited and interviewed 10 people from two age groups (1) Young Millenials & Gen Z and (2) Family members of Group 1 (parents, siblings, and relatives) to identify pain points in their social media usage and journaling habits.
